Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GetAIReply

How does GetAIReply work?

GetAIReply automatically analyzes your customer reviews and generates personalized, professional responses in seconds. Our AI understands the context, tone, and specifics of each review to create authentic responses.

Which platforms are supported?

GetAIReply works with Google Business, TripAdvisor, Booking.com, Airbnb, Facebook, Yelp, Trustpilot, TheFork and many more. Our Chrome extension automatically detects the platform and adapts accordingly.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. GetAIReply is 100% European and GDPR compliant. Your data is hosted in Europe, never resold or used to train AI models. We use end-to-end encryption.

Can I customize the response tone?

Yes! You can configure the tone (formal, friendly, professional), response length, and add specific information about your business that the AI will automatically incorporate.

Is the free trial really free?

Yes, 100% free. You get 5 generations per month with no credit card required. No commitment, no surprises. Upgrade to Pro whenever you want.

How do I install the Chrome extension?

It's simple: click "Add to Chrome" from the Chrome Web Store, sign in to your GetAIReply account, and you're ready! The extension automatically appears on your review platforms.
